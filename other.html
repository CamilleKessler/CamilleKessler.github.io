Sexism in academia

It's 2023, people!

29 November 2023

Before bringing you my very own non-exhaustive list of sexist remarks, situations etc..., I would like to acknowldge the privilege I have in being a white, straight, cis-gender, able, Swiss woman. Sexism is obviously part of my everyday life but I probably wouldn't be where I am if I had not be as priviledged.

Turtle nesting season volunteer

Can turtles be cuter? I don't think so.

19 February 2024

Turtle poaching is still a regular occurence in many countries where sea turtles come ashore to nest. In 2016, I had the chance to volunteer for Project Biodiversity in Cabo Verde, to help in the conservation of the loggerhead sea turtle. It was a really fun experience and I learned a lot, we patrolled the beaches at night to deter potential poachers, recorded nesting turtle activity, monitored the nests, etc... One memorable experience involves me taking the eggs while the turtle is laying, to relocate the nest. Some beaches are unsafe for baby turtles and nests sometimes need to be moved as soon as possible, the soonest being when the mother is in trance, laying. There is something special about spending the whole night in the company of turtle hatchlings and watching the sun rise on the beach, the time stands still, you feel extremely small.
