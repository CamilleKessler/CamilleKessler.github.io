Sexism in academia

It's 2023, people!

29 November 2023

Before bringing you my very own non-exhaustive list of sexist remarks, situations etc..., I would like to acknowldge the privilege I have in being a white, straight, cis-gender, able, Swiss woman. Sexism is obviously part of my everyday life but I probably wouldn't be where I am if I had not be as priviledged.

  • That time when reviewer 3 mansplained
  • That time when a member of the audience joked about me going back to the kitchen
  • That time when the editor replied to my supervisor instead of me
  • That time when a member of the audience interrupted me then went on to say it was a joke
  • That time when I was not given authorship
  • That time when my colleague condescendingly "forgot" to rectify a mistake I had caught
  • That time when a presented made unbelievable assumptions about rape and incest
  • That time in the field when a collegue was flabbergasted a woman could build a fire

Undoubtedly more to come, stay tuned.
